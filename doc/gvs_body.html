Introduction

The GVS software packaged by the TRMM Office is a major component of GVBOX.  It consists of three levels of packages: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 as specified by the TRMM Science Data and Information System (TSDIS). Each level has a set of programs that produce products for that level. Level 1 programs, typically, ingest level 0 files and produce a level 1 product. Level 2 programs, typically, ingest level 1 files and produce a level 2 product. Similarily for level 3 programs. 

Level 1 Programs

level_1: GV Calibrated radar data and Quality Controlled Radar Reflectivity (Rosenfeld, Wolff, et al.)

Level 2 Programs

2A-52i: Rain existence (Wolff et al.)

2A-53: Rain rate map (Merritt and Yuter)

2A-54: Convective/Stratiform Map (Steiner et al.)

2A-54-dc: Convective/Stratiform Map from Dual CAPPI (Steiner et al.)

2A-55: 3-d reflectivity (Steiner et al.)

2A-56: Rain gauge (Merritt)

2A-57: Disdrometer (Merritt)

Level 3 Programs

3A-53: 5-Day Site Rainfall Map (Brodzik)

3A-54: Monthly Site Rainfall Map (Brodzik)

3A-55: Monthly 3-D Structure (Merritt)
